MegaGlest Tactical Scenarios

Genre:Real-Time Tactics
Perspective:Top-down
Players:1

Death Road

  • Download: death_road.zip
  • Forum thread: TBA

You must guide a man along a deadly path so that he can reach the castle safely. Unfortunately, his guards are severely outnumbered and outgunned.

  • Player faction: Tech
  • Enemy faction: Magic, Indian
  • Tileset: Dark Forest
  • Difficulty: Hard
  • Engine: MegaGlest
  • License: GNU GPL

Native Unrest

  • Download: native_unrest.zip
  • Forum thread: https://forum.megaglest.org/index.php?topic=9921.0

In this scenario, you take command of a disgruntled Indian tribe. Your mission is to destroy the castle to the north-East, putting an end to the king's reign. But the only path to the king's castle is heavily guarded, and you are outnumbered.

  • Player faction: Indian
  • Enemy faction: Tech
  • Tileset: Winter Forest
  • Difficulty: Hard
  • Engine: MegaGlest
  • License: GNU GPL

Warlock Overlords

  • Download: warlock_overlords.zip
  • Forum thread: https://forum.megaglest.org/index.php?topic=9920.0

This is a simple one. You take control of four archmages and have to use them to destroy four settlements.

  • Player faction: Indian
  • Enemy faction: Tech
  • Tileset: Winter Forest
  • Difficulty: Hard
  • Engine: MegaGlest
  • License: GNU GPL
