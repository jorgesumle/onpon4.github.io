reTux

Genre:Action
Perspective:Side-scrolling
Players:1
License:GNU GPLv3+

ReTux is an action platformer loosely inspired by the Mario games, utilizing the art assets from the SuperTux project. The name "ReTux" is a play on the words "redux" and "Tux".

The story of ReTux is not finalized yet. Basically, it's going to be something about the snow people, led by Raccot the yeti, taking over Icy Island.

ReTux is my first game that I am trying to commercialize. The way I am attempting to do this is with crowdfunding, which I am using as a way to sell the game to the public. If the campaign fails, there's a fallback: anyone who contributes $20 or more will get a copy of the game, both at the end of the campaign and when the game is finished. The game will of course be 100% libre software and libre culture no matter what. The crowdfunding campaign (hosted by IndieGogo) can be found here:

http://igg.me/at/retux
