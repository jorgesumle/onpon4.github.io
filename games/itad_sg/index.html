ITAD S.G.

Genre:Action
Perspective:Top-down
Players:1-2
License:GNU GPLv3+

ITAD S.G. (an acronym which stands for "Is This Another Dumb Space Game?") is a scrolling shooter that I intended to target the OpenPandora. I scrapped it because it seemed unnecessary to add yet another scrolling shooter to the system.

This was where I first tried to do something like the SGE Game Engine. At the time, I tried to do it as a template that used Pygame. It wasn't bad; I could have adapted the template to some other game fairly easily (and was planning on this before I started on the SGE), but it was far from perfect. It was a fairly manual effort and was somewhat difficult to maintain, because Pygame was still interfaced with at a low level, and everything was compacted into a single file.
