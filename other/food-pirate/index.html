Food Pirate

Status:Complete
Category:Comics
License:CC BY-SA 3.0
Description:Parody of the terms "pirate" and "piracy" as it is commonly used to describe unauthorized copying.

Transcript

Man: "I have a confession to make. I am guilty of PIRACY."

Instead of legally purchasing Nabisco's Triscuit woven wheat crackers, I baked my own.

Instead of legally purchasing Nabisco's Oreo sandwitch cookies, I made my own.

Instead of legally purchasing Nabisco's Chips Ahoy! chocolate chip cookies, I baked my own, and mine were much better than theirs!

I did all of this without ever paying Nabisco a dime in royalties, and to top it all off, I gave these home-made baked goods to my friends. In total, I stole $10,000 of potential profit from Nabisco.

Man (behind bars): "I am now rightfully in jail for my horrible actions. So remember, kids: food piracy is a CRIME. Don't do it."
