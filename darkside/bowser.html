Bowser: The Betrayal

Bowser: The Betrayal was my first game, discounting a few basic scrolling shooters. It was made with the proprietary Game Maker 6 game engine, and I never released the source code. It was scrapped a couple months after I started it due to lack of interest.

Bowser: The Betrayal was a Mario fangame with Bowser as the protagonist. It was supposed to take place directly after the events of Super Mario Bros 3. The story centered around Mario turning all non-koopa soldiers against you.

The game itself was not very good on its own merits; the engine was badly programmed, leading to various collision bugs, and Bowser was not able to move very fast or jump very high, making the game boring.

As far as I know, no copies of this game exist anymore. The only evidence that it ever existed at all is its topic on the Game Maker Community.
