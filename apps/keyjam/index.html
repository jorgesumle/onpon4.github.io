Keyjam Tester

Category:Utility
License:Apache License 2.0
Description:Simple utility for testing what keys keyjam.

Just a small utility I wrote so I could check for keyjamming on keyboards. It simply displays what key presses are detected. It was inspired by a similar program distributed with Star Control and Star Control II. Requires Pygame.
